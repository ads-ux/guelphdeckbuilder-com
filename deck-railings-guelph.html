Deck Stairs & Railings in Guelph

Custom stairs and railing systems — safe, code-compliant, and great-looking.

Railings and stairs are where safety and style meet. We build and upgrade railing systems and custom stairs for new and existing decks across Guelph.

Railing options

  • Wood — classic and economical, painted or stained to match.
  • Aluminum — slim, durable, and maintenance-free.
  • Glass panel — unobstructed views, ideal for elevated decks.
  • Cable — modern, minimal, and view-friendly.

Custom stairs

We build code-compliant stairs with proper rise and run, solid stringers, and grippable handrails — including wide entertaining stairs and landings for multi-level decks.

Railing upgrades

Wobbly or non-code railing is the most common deck safety issue we see. Swapping tired wood for aluminum, glass, or cable instantly modernizes a deck and brings it up to code — often as part of a repair and refinish.
