Composite Decking in Guelph

Low-maintenance, rot-proof decking that thrives in Ontario’s climate.

Composite decking is the top choice for Guelph homeowners who want a deck they can enjoy instead of maintain. We install Trex, TimberTech, and Fiberon over properly built pressure-treated framing.

Why composite in Guelph

  • No staining or sealing — ever. Just an occasional wash.
  • Won’t rot, warp, or splinter through freeze-thaw cycles.
  • Fade- and stain-resistant colour that lasts, with long manufacturer warranties.
  • Pairs perfectly with aluminum, glass, or cable railing.

Composite vs pressure-treated

Composite costs more up front but eliminates the yearly maintenance and the replacement of rotted boards down the road. For many homeowners the lifetime cost and the time saved make it the better value — see our cost breakdown.

Done right under the boards

A composite surface is only as good as the frame beneath it. We build code-compliant, frost-depth-footed structures so your composite deck stays flat and solid for its full lifespan.
