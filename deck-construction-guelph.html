New Deck Construction in Guelph

Custom decks designed, permitted, and built for Guelph and Wellington County homes.

We design and build custom decks from the footings up — single-level, multi-level, wrap-around, and decks integrated with stairs, railings, and pergolas. Every build is engineered for Guelph’s freeze-thaw climate and constructed to the Ontario Building Code.

What’s included

  • Free on-site design consultation and a detailed written quote.
  • Permit drawings and application handled for you.
  • Proper frost-depth footings, pressure-treated structural framing, and code-compliant guards.
  • Your choice of pressure-treated, cedar-alternative, or composite decking.
  • Custom stairs and railings — wood, aluminum, glass, or cable.

Materials we build with

Pressure-treated lumber is the proven budget choice; composite (Trex, TimberTech, Fiberon) is the low-maintenance premium option that holds up beautifully in Ontario winters. We help you choose based on budget, look, and how much upkeep you want.

Built for Guelph

Sloped lots, clay soil, and deep frost are all normal here, and they all affect how a deck should be footed and framed. Local experience means your deck stays level, solid, and safe for decades.
