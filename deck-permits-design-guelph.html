Deck Permits & Design in Guelph

We handle the drawings and the City of Guelph permit, start to finish.

Permits and drawings are the part most homeowners dread — so we take care of them. From concept to approved plans, we manage the design and the City of Guelph building permit for your project.

Design

We start with a free on-site consultation to understand your yard, how you’ll use the space, and your budget. You get design drawings and a detailed written quote before any commitment.

Permit application

Most Guelph decks need a permit — attached decks, anything over 24 inches above grade, and decks larger than ~108 sq ft (see our permit guide). We prepare the site plan and construction drawings to Ontario Building Code and submit the application for you. Approval typically takes 2–4 weeks.

Built to the approved plans

Once the permit is issued, we build exactly to the approved drawings and coordinate the required inspections — so your finished deck is fully legal and ready for resale down the road.
