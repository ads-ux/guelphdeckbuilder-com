Deck Repair & Refinishing in Guelph

Make an aging deck safe, solid, and good-looking again.

Not every deck needs replacing. We repair and refinish structurally sound decks across Guelph — and we’ll tell you honestly when a deck is past the point of safe repair.

Common repairs we do

  • Replacing rotten, cracked, or warped deck boards.
  • Fixing wobbly or non-code railings and loose stairs.
  • Re-securing or replacing failing ledger and post connections.
  • Sanding, cleaning, staining, and sealing tired surfaces.

Is your deck safe?

Warning signs include springy or soft boards underfoot, wobbly railings, rot at post bases or where the deck meets the house, and a deck that has visibly pulled away from the wall. We provide a free structural assessment as part of any estimate visit.

Repair or replace?

If the framing and footings are sound, repair and refinishing is the cost-effective route. If the structure is compromised, we’ll quote a new build instead — and explain why.
